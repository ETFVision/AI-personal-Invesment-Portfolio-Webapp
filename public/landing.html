Product philosophy

An analytics workspace — not a trading terminal or advisor.

ETFVision is built around understanding broad, diversified, low-maintenance ETF allocations. It provides analytics, observations, and educational insights — never personalized buy, sell, or rebalancing advice. Disciplined, evidence-based, and built for the long term.

✓ ETF-first lens

Diversification, liquidity, and simplicity at the core — with optional satellite exposures analyzed in context.

✓ Transparent methodology

Clear methodology behind every metric, score, and observation — so you can verify the reasoning.

✓ Portable by design

Vendor-independent and built so your data and workspace stay yours.
